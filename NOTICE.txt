- pandas
- SciPy

No third-party source code is included or redistributed in this repository; the packages
above are used as external dependencies.

Charlton et al. (2021)
----------------------
`rsp_quality_charlton()` implements the impedance pneumography signal quality index
described in:

Charlton PH, Bonnici T, Tarassenko L, Clifton DA, Beale R, Watkinson PJ, Alastruey J.
An impedance pneumography signal quality index: Design, assessment and application to
respiratory rate monitoring. Biomed Signal Process Control. 2021;65:102339.
doi:10.1016/j.bspc.2020.102339. PMID: 34168684. PMCID: PMC7611038.
